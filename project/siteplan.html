Site Plan

Name: Auto Partes Pepe

Reasoning: This name honors a three generation family legacy in the automotive electrical business. It is personal, memorable, and establishes trust within the local community of Falcón.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a digital catalog for a specialized automotive electrical store. It allows customers to verify part availability and submit technical inquiries via a structured form, streamlining the sales process for both the owner and the client.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: "Does Auto Partes Pepe have a 12V high-output alternator for a 2015 Toyota Hilux in stock?"
  • Scenario 2: "I am a local mechanic; how can I send the specific technical details of a faulty starter motor to see if a replacement is available?"

Color Schema

The palette is designed to reflect an industrial, automotive, and high-energy environment.

Primary: Red (Buttons/Links)
Secondary: Grey (Headings/Icons)
Accent: Black (Navigation/Text)

Typography

Font: Roboto (Sans-Serif)

  • Headings: Roboto Bold (700) for a strong, mechanical look.
  • Body Text: Roboto Regular (400) for maximum legibility on technical part numbers.
